# Notice and Attribution

This repository organizes public cloud native conference material for study, research, and knowledge management.

## Primary sources

The vault may reference, summarize, transcribe, or link to content from public sources such as:

- CNCF YouTube channel: https://www.youtube.com/@cncf
- KubeCon + CloudNativeCon event pages and schedules
- Linux Foundation / Sched public agendas
- PromCon public event pages and recordings
- Public playlists, video metadata, abstracts, speaker names, and session pages

## Attribution guidance

When reusing notes, summaries, transcripts, or derived material from this repository, preserve attribution to the original source whenever applicable.

Recommended attribution format:

> Based on public conference material from `[event/session title]`, presented by `[speaker names]` at `[event name/year]`, originally published by CNCF/Linux Foundation or the respective event organizer. Source: `[official URL or YouTube URL]`.

For repository-original maps, notes, structure, or documentation, attribute:

> Cloud Native Second Brain by Ezequiel Barros, licensed under CC BY 4.0.

## Creative Commons videos

Some source videos may be published under a Creative Commons license, commonly Creative Commons Attribution (CC BY). In those cases, derived material should preserve the attribution required by the source license.

Do not assume that every linked or indexed source has the same license. Check the original source page before redistributing full transcripts, slides, audio, video, or large derived excerpts.
