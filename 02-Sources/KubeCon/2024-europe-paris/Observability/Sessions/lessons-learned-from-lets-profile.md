## Descrição oficial

On the Polar Signals livestream "Let's Profile" a lot of cloud-native software has been taken under a microscope by thoroughly profiling it in production and attempting to save the world CPU cycles, and therefore electricity and making the world a little better. This included containerd, Prometheus node-exporter, Kubernetes, kubectl, OpenTelemetry collector, Flux and more. Based on this a number of themes have emerged of what engineers and operators should look out for and more generally how profiling can be used to understand our applications better and reduce unnecessary CPU cycles. Join this session to learn how to understand your applications and infrastructure at unprecedented depth using profiling and the Parca open-source project just like we've done with lots of cloud native software already!
