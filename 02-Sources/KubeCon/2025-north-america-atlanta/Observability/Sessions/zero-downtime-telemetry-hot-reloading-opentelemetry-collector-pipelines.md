## Resumo

Cloud-native systems demand real-time adaptability—yet updating OpenTelemetry Collector processors often means restarts, risking data loss and breaking observability. In this talk, we introduce a powerful hot reload mechanism for dynamic reconfiguration of processors like filters, samplers, and transformers—without ever restarting the collector. Attendees will learn how to architect hot-swappable processors that respond instantly to pipeline changes, enabling seamless updates, uninterrupted data flow, and zero downtime.

## Descrição oficial

Cloud-native systems demand real-time adaptability—yet updating OpenTelemetry Collector processors often means restarts, risking data loss and breaking observability. 
 
 In this talk, we introduce a powerful hot reload mechanism for dynamic reconfiguration of processors like filters, samplers, and transformers—without ever restarting the collector. 
 
 Attendees will learn how to architect hot-swappable processors that respond instantly to pipeline changes, enabling seamless updates, uninterrupted data flow, and zero downtime. Whether you're managing complex telemetry at scale or just tired of fragile restarts, this session delivers the blueprint for a more resilient, continuously observable stack.
