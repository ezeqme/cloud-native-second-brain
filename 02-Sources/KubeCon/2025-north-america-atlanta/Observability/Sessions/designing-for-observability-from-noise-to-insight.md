## Descrição oficial

Engineers often face cognitive overload, noisy dashboards, and alert fatigue, especially when working with complex tools like OpenTelemetry. These challenges aren’t just technical; they’re usability failures that impact incident response and developer experience. Yet the success of observability platforms often hinges on something less visible: design. Behind every intuitive dashboard and frictionless workflow is a designer translating complexity into clarity.

This session offers a behind-the-scenes look from a product designer working in observability. Drawing on real-world experience designing for Kubernetes-native platforms built on OpenTelemetry, the speaker shares how design decisions shape engineers’ ability to understand, trust, and act on telemetry data, especially during high-pressure incidents.

Takeaways: Why design is critical to observability and DevEx, plus actionable strategies for visualizing complex telemetry.
